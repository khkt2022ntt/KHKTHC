EXHIBITION AREAS 1

Exhibition area 1 displays and introduces the spiritual culture of the Khmer people in Tra Vinh

Exhibition area 1 displays and introduces the spiritual culture of the Khmer people in Tra Vinh, in which the Khmer Theravada Buddhist pagodas play a leading and unique role. In the most solemn position reproduces part of the main hall of the Khmer Theravada Buddhist pagoda; the Dharma chair is covered when the senior monk holds a sermon on festive occasions; images introduce the system of 143 Khmer pagodas in Tra Vinh; The model of Sala worshiping Neak Ta and the artifacts are valuable stone sculptures, bearing the imprint of Brahmanism and Buddhism, reflecting the development of spiritual life in the history of Khmer people in the South in general, Khmer people in Tra Vinh in particular, such as Linga-Yoni, Mukhalinga, God statues (Shiva, Vishnu), Avalokitesvara, Lokesvara, Buddha statues...
