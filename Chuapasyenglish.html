PY-SEY VA-RA-RAM PAGODA

Ba Sy Pagoda

On 18/72012, the Ministry of Culture, Sports and Tourism issued Decision No. 2631/QD-BVHTTDL ranking Pyseyvaram Pagoda as a national relic of historical type.

Address

About 10 km to the northwest of Tra Vinh city center, about 13 km southeast of Cang Long town in Giong Chua, Phuong Thanh commune, Cang Long district, Tra Vinh province.

History of Construction

Built at the end of the 17th century, there have been 11 generations of abbots. During the resistance wars against foreign invaders, Ba Sy Pagoda was also a place to nurture local revolutionary leaders.

Architecture

The pagoda has a 6-hectare campus, hidden under the trees reaching an age of hundreds of years old and has an architectural complex including the gate, the main hall, the sala thiene (dining areas), sala pali (Pali school) ... bearing the special architecture and culture of the Khmer people.
