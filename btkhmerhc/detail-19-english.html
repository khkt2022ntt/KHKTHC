Artifact information

The Hanuman monkey god mask is used for the character of Hanuman in the Ro Bam dance of the Khmer epic, Reamker. Hanuman is a character who is loyal, brave, and incredibly strong, who helps Prince Preah Ream rescue Princess Seda from the clutches of the evil King Krong Reap, who had taken her as his wife in the Langka city.
